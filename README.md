# RESTIQ Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 2, 2026**

## Introduction

RESTIQ ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the RESTIQ mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Health Data
With your explicit permission, RESTIQ accesses the following health data from Apple HealthKit:
- Sleep data (duration, sleep stages, sleep quality)
- Workout data (exercise type, duration, heart rate during workouts)
- Heart rate data (resting heart rate, heart rate variability)
- Nutrition data (calories, macronutrients - if you choose to log meals)
- Step count and activity data

**Important:** We do NOT write any data to HealthKit. We only read data you've already tracked.

### Photos
When you use the AI meal estimation feature:
- Photos of meals you choose to capture or select from your photo library
- These photos are temporarily sent to our AI service provider (Google Gemini API) for nutritional analysis
- Photos are stored locally on your device only
- We do NOT store your meal photos on our servers beyond the time needed for AI processing

### Location Data
If you enable weather themes:
- Your approximate location to display weather-specific background themes
- Location data is NOT stored or shared with third parties
- This feature can be disabled at any time in the App settings

### Personal Information
- Display name (optional, stored locally on your device)
- Fitness goals and targets (stored locally on your device)
- App usage data and preferences (stored locally on your device)

## How We Use Your Information

### Local Processing
Most of your data is processed entirely on your device:
- Sleep analysis and scoring
- Workout summaries and trends
- Nutrition tracking and calculations
- Daily health metrics

### AI-Powered Features
When you use AI-powered features, data is sent to our AI service provider:

**Weekly AI Reports:**
- Your aggregated health metrics (sleep scores, workout data, nutrition summary) from the past 30 days
- Sent to Google Gemini API for generating personalized health insights
- Data is NOT permanently stored by the AI provider
- Processing complies with Google's data protection standards

**AI Meal Estimation:**
- Photos of meals you capture
- Sent to Google Gemini API for nutritional analysis
- Photos are deleted after processing (typically within seconds)
- Estimated nutrition data is stored locally on your device

## Data Storage

### On Your Device
The majority of your data is stored locally on your iPhone:
- All health data pulled from HealthKit
- Your meal logs and nutrition entries
- Daily journal entries and check-ins
- App settings and preferences
- Weekly AI reports (after generation)
- Meal photos (stored in the App's local directory)

### On Our Servers
We operate a minimal backend server for:
- AI report generation coordination
- Anonymous usage analytics (app opens, feature usage - no personal health data)
- Crash reporting (to fix bugs and improve the App)

**We do NOT:**
- Store your HealthKit data on our servers
- Sell your data to third parties
- Use your health data for advertising
- Share your personal information with third parties (except AI processing as described above)

## Third-Party Services

### Google Gemini AI
- Used for: Generating weekly AI health reports and estimating meal nutrition from photos
- Data shared: Aggregated health metrics (for reports) and meal photos (for estimation)
- Retention: Data is not permanently stored by Google
- Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Apple HealthKit
- Your health data remains in Apple's HealthKit
- We only read data you've already tracked
- Subject to Apple's Health Data Privacy Policy

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- All data transmitted to our servers uses HTTPS encryption
- Local data on your device is protected by iOS security features
- No passwords or payment information is stored (if we add subscriptions, payments are handled entirely by Apple)
- Regular security audits and updates

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Control
You have full control over your data:
- **Delete Account:** Delete all locally stored data at any time through the App
- **Revoke HealthKit Permissions:** Disable HealthKit access in iOS Settings at any time
- **Disable AI Features:** Turn off AI report generation and meal estimation in App settings
- **Disable Location:** Turn off weather themes to prevent location access
- **Export Your Data:** Request a copy of your data by contacting us

### Data Retention
- Local data: Stored on your device until you delete the App or manually delete data
- AI processing: Temporary (data not retained after processing)
- Server logs: Retained for 90 days for debugging purposes

## Children's Privacy

RESTIQ is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## Medical Disclaimer
